Marketing and Promotion: Through our website we can reach a broader audience by promoting our services through social networks such as Facebook, Instagram and Tiktok, and then direct them to our website to make a purchase.

Audience

What do hey want?

They are looking for thrilling activities to share with their friends.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

They will find us mostly through their mobile devices, as they spend most their time on social media.

What do they expect to find by them accessing the website?

A website where they can view photos and videos of other people who had an incredible time rafting with their friends, so they can show their friends and decide to book the service for themselves.

Conclusion:

Our target audience is young people aged 16 to 29, because they are the type of people who seek out thrilling and adrenaline-fueled activities in their lives. These young adults are our ideal custommers because they are more likely to make purchases on our website.

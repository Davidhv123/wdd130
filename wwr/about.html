Rafting Awesome

Rafting Awesome was born from a passion for adventure and nature. Founded by a group of friends who love rafting, our mission is to offer unforgettable experiences on the most exciting rivers. With experienced guides and high-quality equipment, we guarantee safety and fun for everyone. Join Rafting Awesome and discover the adrenaline and beauty of the rapids. Your next adventure awaits!

History

David Huaroto, passionate about nature and rafting, founded Rafting Awesome in 2015. Since then, we have been offering thrilling experiences on rivers worldwide. In 2018, under David's vision, the company innovated with cutting-edge equipment and highly trained guides. By 2020, new routes and activities were added to provide even more variety and fun to our customers. Rafting Awesome has become synonymous with adventure and excellence in the world of rafting. Join us and experience an unforgettable adventure.
